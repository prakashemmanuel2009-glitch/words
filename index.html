Academy Training Areas

  1. Preclinical Writing: PK-PD, toxicology, and CMC sections
  2. Clinical Writing: CSR, CSP, ICF, IB, CTD overview and summary sections
  3. Safety Writing: PSUR, DSUR, RMP, PADER, patient narratives, product label
  4. Publications Writing: manuscripts, abstracts, posters, slide decks
  5. Patient/Public Writing: lay summaries and medical information in simple English

Coverage includes new chemical entities, generics, biologics, biosimilars, and medical devices.
